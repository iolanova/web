Master the Power of CauseffEcon!

Unlock the Secrets of Economics with CauseffEcon – Your Ultimate Learning Companion!

Explore cause-and-effect-chains in economics, deepen your understanding of global events. Set personal goals and track your progress!

  • Why?
  • For whom?
  • Features

Economics is all around us. A seemingly simple change in a government policy, market fluctuation, or international trade decision can have far-reaching consequences that impact businesses, consumers, and entire economies. From everyday transactions to global financial events, understanding the intricate web of cause-and-effect relationships is key.

Ready?

If you’re ready to explore the fascinating world of economics, CauseffEcon is the perfect app for you. No user tracking, no 3rd party links, no ads, no costs – just play. Simply download and start your journey to mastering cause-and-effect-chains in economics with CauseffEcon—your future self will thank you for it!


Whether you're a student preparing for exams, a professional, or simply curious about how the economic world operates – CauseffEcon is the app to deepen your understanding of the interconnected world of economics.

Students & Learners: This app focuses on helping you preparing for exams, seminars or oral discussions. It will sharpen your cause-and-effect reasoning skills and train a more nuanced and in-depth grasp of economic processes. Whether you’re a high school student or working on advanced coursework, or for the International Baccalaureate (IB), you’ll find the app’s quizzes to be a valuable resource for exam preparation.
By engaging with the quiz levels, you’ll notice, that your responses move beyond basic facts. Instead, you’ll begin to think critically about the underlying mechanisms driving economic events. With each successful quiz, your ability to anticipate consequences, understand market behavior, and connect economic phenomena improves.

Economists & managers: Even beyond education, CauseffEcon is valuable for anyone interested in economics. Training helps practitioners, business owners, and managers to better understand how economic forces shape markets, and industries. For example, when you hear about a government’s fiscal policy or an economic downturn, you’ll be challenged to understand what’s at stake, how it affects different sectors, and what consequences may arise in the future.


Learn at your own pace:
No one is judging you. CauseffEcon is designed to provide a dynamic and engaging learning environment: - Set your own targets to reach and adjust the difficulty to suit your skills. - Choose the ratio of correct and incorrect answers to challenge but not overwhelm you. - Decide whether you’re in "Train Mode" for ongoing practice or "Exam Mode" to simulate a test-like experience.

Choose difficulty levels:

1. Rookie Level – Ideal for beginners. At this level, you’ll cover the basics of economics, including:
- Economic Systems
- Pricing Policies
- Market Failures
- …

2. Pro Level – For those who want to dive deeper into more advanced topics such as:
- Economic Growth & Employment
- Demand and Supply-Oriented Economic Policies
- External Balance & Environmental Issues
- …

3. Expert Level – Get a real-world perspective on global economic events. This level explores actual cases and their ripple effects, giving you the complexities of economics in practice. Choose from topics like:
- World Bank Report 2024
- Financial & Economic Crisis 2008/09
- Impact of Post-COVID-19 De-Globalization
- ...

Content Help: If you get stuck, you can refer to detailed content help that explains the basics and background to the economic event. This ensures that you’re not just memorizing answers but actually understand the deeper connections and mechanisms at play.
Track your progress and celebrate goals: We know that staying motivated is key to long-term learning. That’s why CauseffEcon includes features to track your progress.

Observe how your knowledge grows!
